This is the website for the Shared Music Project in UW CSE 403 Spring 2015. This website is currently under construction.

Shared Music is a social music sharing application based on sharing music through Soundcloud. It allows users to share music quickly, effortlessly, and enjoyably through experiencing music socially in real-time.Users can join a room and share music with their friends through a collaborative play queue that lets everyone listen to the same song at the same time.
